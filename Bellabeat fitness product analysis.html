Bellabeat fitness product analysis case study

Introduction

In this case study,I will be analyzing fitness tracker data for Bellabeat, a high-tech manufacturer of health-focused products for women. The goal is to gain insights into consumer usage of these products, which will inform Bellabeat’s future marketing strategies. To address the key business questions, I will follow the steps of the data analysis process: asking questions, preparing data, processing data, analyzing data, sharing findings, and taking action.

Business Task

Urška Sršen ,cofounder and Chief Creative Officer of Bellabeat, has requested an analysis of smart device usage data to gain insights into how consumers use non-Bellabeat smart devices. The goal is to apply these insights to a specific Bellabeat product and provide recommendations to influence Bellabeat’s marketing strategy. I’ve chosen to focus my

analysis on Leaf, Bellabeat’s classic wellness tracker. This versatile device can be worn as a bracelet, necklace, or clip. The Leaf tracker connects to the Bellabeat app, where it monitors and tracks activity, sleep, and stress levels.
